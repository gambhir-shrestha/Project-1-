About Blood Bridge

Blood Bridge is a platform dedicated to connecting blood donors with those in need. Our mission is to save lives by facilitating easy access to blood donations across Nepal. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to live, and through our platform, we aim to make the process of finding and donating blood as seamless as possible.
